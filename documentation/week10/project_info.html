This diagram represents the principal parts of our project and the main interactions that can be processed. The buttons "square" and "triangle" modify the image displayed. A drag event can take place over the image, and this process will modify the text displayed on the imput text area. This will edit the values of the selected keyframe stored on the keyframes list. It is also posible to introduce the data directly on the imput text area without a frag event. the "add keyframe" and "delete keyframe" access directly to the keyframe list and modify it. For starting the display process we have implemented a button that starts and stops the animation.

For using our animator, first we must choose a time space where place the keyframe. In order to do it, we must move the time slider to the desired position. After touchstart it's necessary to click the "add keyframe" button. Then a new keyframe appears on the list. However, al the data is initialized to standard values, and it is probable that those values aren't what you are looking for. There are two options for editing these values: you can drag the element an drop it on the desired place or just modify the numbers on the text area below the image. If you also want to edit the rotation and/or the scale it is mandatory to change the values on the text area. Once your keyframe is edited, you can repeat the processe as many times as you want. When you want to see the result, just click the start/stop button on the slider.
